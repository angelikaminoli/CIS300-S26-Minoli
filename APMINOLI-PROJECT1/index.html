Welcome to the Villa

At Villa Polyxena, we are dedicated to our craft. Based off the Greek notion of "philoxenia" — the love of strangers - our villa invites you to bask in the warmth of our hospitality and the beauty of our surroundings. Our commitment to excellence extends to the design of the villa, the quality of our food, and the personalized itineraries we craft for each guest. Nestled along the shores of the Pagasetic Gulf, our property offers a seamless blend of traditional Greek architecture and modern luxury. The estate is built on a private beach, overlooking a quiet island, while a short bike ride takes you to the awe-inspiring heritage site of Meteora — one of the most dramatic landscapes in all of Europe. We can't wait to share all that our home has to offer you.

Whether you are seeking a romantic getaway, a special celebration, or to join one of our world-class wellness retreats, Villa Polyxena provides the perfect destination. Our attentive staff, world-class spa, and private chef services ensure that every detail of your stay is handled with care.
