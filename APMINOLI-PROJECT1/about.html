Our Location

Situated on the western shores of the Pagasetic Gulf — a sheltered bay in the heart of the Aegean — Villa Polyxena rests on some of the calmest, clearest waters in all of Greece. The region is rich in mythology and history: the neighboring city of Amaliapolis, and it's miniature island nestled across the horizon, was home to one of the few cities free from Ottoman rule.

Neighboring Volos serves as our gateway city, offering a vibrant waterfront, excellent traditional tavernas, and convenient transport links. The Soprades Islands are a quick boat ride away, perfect for day trips. The spectacular rock monasteries of Meteora, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, are just an hour's drive inland, making our location ideal for guests who wish to combine relaxation with cultural exploration.
